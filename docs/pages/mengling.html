Mengling Qiao

Dr. MENGLING QIAO is currently a a Postdoctoral Research Scientist in the Columbia Water Center, Department of Earth and Environmental Engineering, Columbia University. Her research interests include Urban Geography, Human Mobility, GeoHealth, Computational Social Science, and Disaster Planning. Currently, she is currently focusing on modeling the mobility patterns of human mobility in disaster events, and sensing the sentiment changes of general population in the post-pandemic era. See more in her website.
