Xiaokang Fu

Dr. Xiaokang Fu is a postdoctoral researcher in State Key Laboratory of Information Engineering in Surveying, Mapping and Remote Sensing, Wuhan University. I am visiting fellow at the CGA of Harvard University. He is also an executor of Spatial Search People. His research interests include Geostatistics and Geoinformatics (GIS), Urban Computing, Social Media Data Mining, Emergency Response. See more in his website.
